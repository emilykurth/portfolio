About Me

Emily Kurth

I'm a visual designer and brand thinker from San Diego, focused on turning ideas into clear, intentional visuals. My work includes visual identity, social media graphics, t‑shirt design, and early stage brand strategy, all centered on helping brands find a visual voice that means something.

I've built a foundation in industry tools and digital workflows, working with Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop to create graphics and layout-based pieces. I pay attention to consistency, hierarchy, and typography, and I'm comfortable managing assets and adapting work across different platforms and screen sizes so it stays cohesive.

I'm particularly drawn to early stage and entrepreneurial projects, and I use AI tools and new technology to make my process more efficient, from idea exploration to iteration. I'm still building experience, but I'm motivated by the challenge of taking loose concepts and shaping them into visuals people can see, understand, and get excited about.
